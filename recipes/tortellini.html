Tortellini

Description

Creamy tortellini soup made easy in the instant pot! This recipe is less soup and more entree and pairs well with toasted bread.

Dive in!!!

Ingredients

  • 1T olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 15oz can crushed fire-roasted tomatoes
  • 1/2t dried oregano
  • 1/2t dried basil
  • 1/4t dried thyme
  • 3C vegetable stock
  • 10oz fresh tortellini, cheese-filled
  • 2C baby spinach
  • 1/2C heavy cream

Garnish

  • grated parmesan
  • fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Select the SAUTE setting on Instant Pot. Heat oil, add onion carrot, and garlic. Saute until the onions are soft and translucent.
  2. Switch off the SAUTE setting. Add the diced tomatoes, all the seasonings to include salt and pepper, and broth. Stir everything well.
  3. CLose the lid securely, and turn the vent to SEALING. Select the PRESSURE COOK/MANUAL HIGH PRESSURE setting and set the cooking time to 1 minute.
  4. When the instant pot beeps, allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then release any remaining pressure before removing the lid.
  5. Select the SAUTE setting, and add in the tortellini. Stir and cook for 3-5 minutes.
  6. Stir in the spinach after turning off the heat. The spinach will wilt with help of residual heat.
  7. Finally, add the heavy cream, and give a quick mix. Adjust the seasoning at the step, if necessary.
  8. Add garnish, if using. Serve warm.
