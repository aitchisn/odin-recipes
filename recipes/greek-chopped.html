Chopped Greek Salad

Description

Quick and easy greek salad using fresh veggies, feta cheese, and traditional mediterranean seasonings.

Serve atop a bed of lettuce, if desired, as it does taste great on its own. Other optional add-ins include chickpeas, artichoke hearts, and bowtie pasta.

Get to Choppin'!

Ingredients

  • 4 roma tomatoes or 8oz grape tomatoes
  • 2 cucumbers
  • 1 red onion
  • 8oz kalamata olives, halved and pitted
  • 8oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2T olive oil
  • 2T red wine vinegar
  • 1 lemon, juice
  • Dash of flaked salt
  • Dash of flaked black pepper
  • Pinch of choice herbs (oregano, rosemary, thyme, tarragon, etc.)

Steps

  1. Quarter roma tomatoes or halve grape tomatoes, whichever is used, and add to large bowl.
  2. Peel cucumbers and quarter lengthwise. Slice into 1/2-inch chunks. Add to large bowl.
  3. Peel outer skin of onion, and chop roughly. Add to large bowl.
  4. Drain olives in strainer, do not rinse. Add to large bowl.
  5. Whisk olive oil, red wine vinegar, and lemon juice in separate small bowl. Once combined, add dressing to large bowl.
  6. Add salt, pepper, and choice herbs to taste.
  7. Add feta crumbles to salad.
  8. Carefully mix to combine and serve cold.
