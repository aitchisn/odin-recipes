Easy Chia Pudding

Description

Delicious start to your day that's ready overnight!

This pudding is made with almond milk, chia and flax seeds, and can include any fresh toppings you desire!

Ingredients

  • 1/4C almond milk
  • 2T chia seeds
  • 1T flaxseed, ground

Optional

  • Choice berries
  • Dab of honey
    •

Steps

  1. Pour almond milk into a small sealable container.
  2. Add chia seeds and flaxseed to milk, stir to combine.
  3. Seal container and place in refrigerator overnight.
  4. Add optional desired toppings and enjoy!
